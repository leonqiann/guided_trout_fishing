Our rates offer flexible options for your fishing trip. The standard rate is $250 including GST per day per guide, suitable for up to two anglers, providing personalized service.

If you'd like to include boat transport, the rate remains $250 including GST per day per guide and can accommodate up to four anglers, making it perfect for small groups.

We also offer helicopter transport to remote fishing spots for $250 including GST per day per guide, ideal for up to two anglers seeking an exclusive experience.

All packages include necessary equipment and safety gear for your comfort and safety. These rates are based on daily bookings and can be customized to meet your needs. For additional options or special arrangements, please contact John Hamilton directly. He will be happy to assist you in planning a memorable and tailored fishing adventure. We look forward to helping you enjoy an unforgettable experience on the water!
